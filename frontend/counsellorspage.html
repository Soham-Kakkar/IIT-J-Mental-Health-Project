Himanshi Singh (RCI Licensed Clinical Psychologist)

About

Himanshi Singh is RCI licensed Clinical Psychologist, working at IIT Jammu since 2021. She did her M.Phil in Clinical Psychology from PGIMS, Rohtak, Haryana. She had an experience of working with AIIMS, New Delhi.

She has been extensively trained in various forms of psychological assessments and psychotherapy including individual therapy (eg., Cognitive behavior therapy, mindfulness, behavior therapy and family therapy. She has expertise in providing therapy for depression, anxiety, academic stress, OCD, adjustment, substance use (tobacco, alcohol, cannabis, opioids, etc.), trauma, anger issues, relationship issues, issues related to transition and change, uncertainties about goals, concentration issues, etc. Along with on-site intervention, she is also well-versed with the online mode of therapy.

Her approach utilizes empathetic and a non-judgemental attitude to create a safe space for individuals. As a therapist, she incorporates individual centric and strength-based practices to provide holistic intervention, while abiding by the highest ethical principles.

Room No: 101

Email: himanshi.singh@iitjammu.ac.in

Nandita Sharma (RCI Licensed Educational Psychologist)

About

Nandita Sharma is RCI Licensed Clinical Psychologist and Educational Psychologist. She has extensive experience as an Academic Researcher and Practicing Psychologist at a medical institution, NGOs, and schools.

Her area of expertise lies in dealing with stress, restlessness, self-esteem, relationship issues, addiction, grief, trauma, LGBTQIA+, anger issues, and procrastination. She is proficient in providing individual counseling & psychotherapy and administering and interpreting psychological assessments. She treats adolescents and adults in a unique environment while incorporating mindfulness, cognitive-behavioral, client-centric, and other strategies as appropriate.

She has also conducted seminars, awareness programs, and workshops for individuals, teachers, and working professionals.

Room No: 102

Email: nandita.sharma@iitjammu.ac.in
